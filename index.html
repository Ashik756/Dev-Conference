Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about DEVCONF 2026.

What is included in the ticket price?

Depending on your tier, tickets include access to all keynote sessions, technical tracks, hands-on workshops, networking lunch, coffee breaks, and an exclusive conference swag bag.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes, all technical talks and keynotes will be recorded. Standard ticket holders get 30 days of digital access, while Pro and Team ticket holders receive lifetime access.

Can I upgrade my ticket later?

Yes, you can upgrade your ticket from Standard to Pro at any time before August 15, 2026, by paying the price difference, subject to ticket availability.

Are there team or group discounts?

Yes! Our Team plan offers a discounted flat rate for up to 10 people, which includes dedicated team lounge access, company logo placement, and priority support.
